《夏洛的网》读后感

发表于 2023-10-02 分类于 读书笔记
本文字数： 1.2k 阅读时长 ≈ 1 分钟

This semester, I read a book named “Charlotte’s Web “written by E.B.White. The book talks about a moving story which is one of the most beautiful memory in my mind.

The story tells a Pig called Wilbur and a spider called Charlotte .They get on well with each other so that they built sincere friendship. However, bad news suddenly came: Wilbur will be killed on Christmas day! To save Wilbur’s life, Charlotte devoted all her lifetime and asked no rewards to create a masterpiece of spider web until the moment she died.

The most impressive episode of this book is the true friendship between Charlotte and Wilbur. A spider is a threat to a pig at first glance, but loneliness suddenly makes Wilbur be familiar with Charlotte and surprisingly he found that this spider is extraordinarily warmhearted.

What strikes me most is Charlotte’s words: “You never die, I’ll save you!” when Wilbur was saying “I don’t want to die!”

After I had read the book, l was in contemplative mood. Only leaving the selfless love and pure kindness, we need such kind of friendship in our life, so we must learn from Charlotte. As they old saying goes’ ‘A friend in need is a friend indeed”. We should help our friends as much as we could when they needed. When this sort of emotion has already been lacked seriously in contemporary society, this story undoubtedly moves the readers.

So, I strongly recommend this book to readers. We should have a heart of enthusiasm and kindness.
